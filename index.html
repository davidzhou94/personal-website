Hello!

My name is David Zhou and I am a Software Development Engineer at Amazon Robotics. I graduated in June 2016 from McGill University in Montréal where I studied computer science and economics. My family is originally from Shandong province in China but I grew up in Markham, Ontario. My personal interests (aside from computer science and economics) include recent history, not-so-recent history, geography (and more specifically maps), public transit, current events, and watching youtube videos of cats and dogs. The most interesting job I had was when I served in the Canadian Border Service as an officer in the postal stream where I protected my fellow Canadians from anabolic steroids, explosives, and not paying their taxes.
